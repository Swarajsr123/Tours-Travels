15 Best Honeymoon Destinations In India In November (2023)

What better way to celebrate the beginning of a new life than to travel with your one and only? We’ve picked 15 best honeymoon destinations in India in May for you and your loved one!

Srinagar, Kashmir

Think about heaven on Earth and Srinagar in Kashmir fits in perfectly. Srinagar is popularly known among newlyweds for wide stretches of Mughal Gardens, mighty mountains covered in blankets of white and ‘shikaras’ floating in dainty lakes. One among the many things you must do in Srinagar is renting a shikara houseboat for a night or two and making the most out of your romantic time here.

Places to visit in Srinagar include the Dal Lake, Nishat Bagh, Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden, Pari Mahal, Shalimar Bagh and many more!

Nearest Airport: Srinagar Airport

Ideal duration of your honeymoon: 5 to 8 days

Also read: Srinagar, The Abode of Things Ethereal

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Shimla was the prime centre of all summer fun things in the British era. The streets of Shimla reflect the brilliance of British architecture even today. Popularly known as the Queen of Hills, Shimla is wrapped around with scenic mountain routes and the very famous Mall Road. While the Mall Road experiences all the touristy buzz, wooden hotels and cottages established amidst the serenity of the valleys of Shimla offer couples some quality time in the lap of nature.

Places to visit in Shimla include the Christ Church, the Shimla Reserve Forest Sanctuary, Kufri and many more!

Nearest Airport: Jubarhati Airport

Ideal duration of your honeymoon: 2 to 3 days

Also read: Shimla, The Abode of Tranquility

Mussoorie, Uttarakhand

Located at a distance of 35 kilometres from Dehradun, Mussoorie is one among the best honeymoon places in India to visit in May and the reasons are definitely convincing! Mussoorie gets its name from the Masur plant that grows in large numbers in this region. Its serenity is comforting newlyweds the much needed escape they need from the daily hustle bustle.

Places to visit in Mussoorie include the Mall Road, Kempty Falls, the Mussoorie Lake, Gun Hill and many others!

Nearest Airport: Dehradun Airport

Ideal duration of your honeymoon: 2 to 3 days

Also read: Mussoorie, The Enchanting Beauty with Tranquil Surroundings

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Darjeeling is the most popular destination when it comes to choosing honeymoon places in India in May. The breath-taking view of the sunrise from the Tiger Hill and a serene Toy Train ride along the twisty turns and picturesque landscapes are some amazing things you can do in Darjeeling. The generosity of nature is abundant in this destination and this is what attracts most newly married couples.

Major tourist attractions in Darjeeling include Basista Loop, Nathu La, Mirik and last but not the least, tea gardens. Get more details about the same in West Bengal Tour Packages.

Nearest Airport: Bagdodra Airport

Ideal duration of your honeymoon: 5 to 8 days
